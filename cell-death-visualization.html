Key Cellular Components

Apoptotic Bodies: Membrane-bound cell fragments containing intact organelles
Phagocyte: Macrophage removing apoptotic bodies without inflammation
Cell Debris: Released intracellular contents from membrane rupture
Inflammatory Cells: Neutrophils and monocytes responding to DAMPs
Scar Tissue: Collagen deposition by fibroblasts replacing normal tissue
